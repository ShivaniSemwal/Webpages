Physics 4400: Statistical Mechanics

Winter 2012

Course information

Text: Daniel V. Schroeder, An Introduction to Thermodynamics, Addison-Wesley Longman (2000) ISBN 0 201 38027 7

Classes: T,Th 15:30-16:45 in C3067

Prerequisites: Physics 3400, Physics 3750

Topics: Ensembles. Classical and quantum statistical mechanics. Statistical mechanics of phase transitions. Advanced topics in statistical mechanics.
